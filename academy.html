LEARNING PATHS

Three Tracks. One Aligned Organization.

Every role in your company needs a different depth of AI understanding. We built three distinct tracks so leadership, operations, and technical teams all reach fluency at the level that matters for their decisions.

Executive Track

AI Strategy for Leaders

Built for C-suite and senior leadership. Covers AI opportunity assessment, investment frameworks, risk management, and how to evaluate vendors without needing to understand the math. Outcome: leaders who ask the right questions and fund the right initiatives.

4 sessions 2 weeks
Operational Track

AI for Operations & Managers

Designed for department heads and process owners. Covers identifying automation candidates, managing AI-augmented workflows, data quality ownership, and change management. Outcome: managers who can scope, champion, and sustain AI within their teams.

6 sessions 3 weeks
Technical Track

AI Implementation Practicum

For IT leads, data teams, and technical staff. Covers model selection, data pipeline architecture, prompt engineering, integration patterns, testing, and deployment. Outcome: builders who can ship production AI systems with confidence.

8 sessions 4 weeks
